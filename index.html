Eggplant Caponata

Eggplant caponata is a tasty relish or appetizer that can be eaten either warm or cold. Best served with toasted crusty bread.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a delicious dish consisting of a slow cooked meat-based sauce on top of cooked spaghetti.

Tiramisu

With its delicious coffee flavor and gently sweetened mascarpone, classic tiramisu will never go out of style. Ado Campeol, a well-known Italian restaurateur, is credited with creating it in the 1970s.
