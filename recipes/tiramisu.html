Tiramisu

Description

With its delicious coffee flavor and gently sweetened mascarpone, classic tiramisu will never go out of style. Ado Campeol, a well-known Italian restaurateur, is credited with creating it in the 1970s.

Ingredients/h3> (Yields 12 servings)
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 1¼ cups heavy cream
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese, at room temperature
  • ¼ cup strong brewed coffee, at room temperature
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
